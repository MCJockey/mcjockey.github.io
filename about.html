About Us

Your partner in support services

Seithati facilities is a company registered in Lesotho to provide facilities management solutions and supplies to residential homes, commercial buildings, and other organisations in general. The company was established in 2023 by the founders Rethabile Ntoampe and Katleho Moshesha.

We deliver value through sustainable and innovative facilities management solutions and supplies that are scalable while ensuring safe and efficient performance of the built environment.

We serve property owners and tenants of the built environment; at the residential or commercial level. We serve men, women, children, youth; and families; young or aged, all of whom trust Seithati to keep their environments clean and safe.

Our investors hold long-term objectives and are interested in longevity of our business. This is reflected in all our operations - we pride ourselves in providing long-lasting value for our clients and long-term relationships. We are also accountable to the environment we operate in; we source and operate in a sustainable ways to ensure that the environment is safe for the future generations.
